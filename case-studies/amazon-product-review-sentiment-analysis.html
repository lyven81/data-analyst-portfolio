Amazon Product Review Sentiment Analysis

Unlocking customer feedback to improve products and marketing strategies in the e-commerce sector.

Executive Summary

This case study explores sentiment analysis of Amazon product reviews within the e-commerce sector, categorizing customer feedback into positive, negative, or neutral sentiments...

Problem Statement

The business sought to understand customer sentiments across various product categories on Amazon to refine product offerings, enhance marketing strategies, and improve overall customer satisfaction...

Approach

Sentiment analysis was conducted on customer reviews using tools such as NLTK and TextBlob...

Visualization: Bar Chart of Sentiment Distribution

Visualization: Word Cloud of Positive Reviews

Results

Positive Sentiment Dominance

Insight: The analysis shows that over 70 reviews are classified as "positive," indicating strong customer satisfaction, particularly in the jewelry category...

Negative Sentiment Insights

Insight: Approximately 25 reviews are classified as "negative." These reviews highlight issues such as product quality, durability, and customer service...

Word Cloud Analysis

Visualization: Word Cloud of Star Rating Positive Sentiment

Product Quality and Initial Experience

Insight: The word cloud for negative sentiment reviews indicates concerns about product durability...

Recommendations

  • Product Quality Improvement: Address quality issues highlighted in negative reviews...
  • Leverage Positive Feedback in Marketing: Utilize strong positive sentiment in marketing campaigns...
