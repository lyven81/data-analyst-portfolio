Forecasting Beverage Sales in Kota Kinabalu During the Chinese New Year Season

Leveraging descriptive and predictive analytics to optimize inventory and promotions during high-demand periods like Chinese New Year.

Executive Summary

This case study analyzes beverage sales performance across store locations in Kota Kinabalu during key events such as Chinese New Year, using descriptive and predictive analytics. The analysis revealed that beverages like Spritzer 1.5L consistently perform well during these high-demand periods. A linear regression model was employed to forecast future sales, allowing inventory and promotional strategies to be optimized. The model achieved an average prediction error (RMSE) of 276.51.

Problem Statement

Retailers face challenges in predicting sales performance during seasonal events, leading to missed opportunities for optimizing inventory and promotions. This study aims to forecast beverage sales during Chinese New Year in Kota Kinabalu, enabling retailers to make informed decisions on stocking and marketing.

Approach

Dataset Overview: The dataset includes transaction-level sales data during major festivals across multiple store locations in Kota Kinabalu.

Study Framework: SQL for business intelligence was used to identify total sales, top-performing products, and the impact of promotions. A linear regression model was built to predict future sales based on promotions, day of the week, and seasonal events.

Descriptive and Predictive Analytics: Historical data was analyzed to build the model, which was evaluated using RMSE to measure its accuracy.

Results

Top-Performing Products and Store Locations

Spritzer 1.5L: Generated the highest sales in Kota Kinabalu during Chinese New Year, with 358 units sold, earning $2,737.32.

Pepsi 330ml: Second-highest in sales, generating $1,307.75 from 330 units sold.

Key Store Locations: Kota Kinabalu emerged as the top-performing location, with total sales of $52,178.38 during the festive period.

Predictive Model Performance

RMSE (Root Mean Squared Error): The model achieved an RMSE of 276.51, indicating a reasonably accurate forecast for future sales.

Key Predictors: Promotions, major festivals, and specific store locations were identified as significant factors impacting sales performance.

Insights on Sales Trends

Significant sales spikes occurred during Chinese New Year, particularly for beverages such as Spritzer and Milo.

Coca-Cola 330ml: Underperformed in all store locations, suggesting a need for revised marketing strategies.

Key Insights

  • Sales Trends During Festive Periods: Spritzer 1.5L and Milo 1L perform well during Chinese New Year. Retailers should focus on stocking these products.
  • Predictive Model Accuracy: The model’s RMSE of 276.51 indicates reliable forecasts for future sales, allowing businesses to anticipate demand.
  • Importance of Promotions: Promotions are a crucial factor in driving sales, as evidenced by the performance of top-selling products.

Recommendations

  • Focus on High-Performing Products: Increase inventory and marketing for top-selling beverages such as Spritzer 1.5L and Milo 1L during festive periods.
  • Optimize Promotions: Tailor promotional strategies based on the predictive model to maximize revenue during high-demand periods.
  • Revise Strategy for Low-Performing Products: Reevaluate marketing strategies for underperforming products like Coca-Cola 330ml.

Conclusion

By applying descriptive and predictive analytics, retailers can optimize inventory and promotional strategies during high-demand periods like Chinese New Year. The predictive model's accuracy provides a reliable basis for forecasting future sales, enabling businesses to make informed decisions and maximize revenue.
