Sentiment Analysis on Jewelry Products

Date: 2023-09-10

Analyzed customer feedback from Amazon to uncover insights for enhancing products and marketing strategies.

  • Goal: Understand customer sentiments to refine product offerings and boost satisfaction.
  • Approach: Applied sentiment analysis using NLTK and TextBlob; visualized findings with bar charts and word clouds.
  • Outcome: Most reviews were positive, particularly for jewelry, while areas for product improvement were also identified.

Analyzing Airbnb Pricing and Availability

Date: 2023-08-25

Optimized pricing strategies for Airbnb listings in Seattle to maximize revenue while balancing occupancy rates.

  • Goal: Develop optimal pricing strategies to improve occupancy and revenue.
  • Approach: Analyzed pricing and availability data to identify trends and opportunities for hosts.
  • Outcome: Found a pricing sweet spot between $100 and $138 where occupancy rates were well-balanced.
