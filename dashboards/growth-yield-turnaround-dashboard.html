What the engines reveal

Empire grew on volume (+RM25.6M), but the cost of opening outlets gave back most of it (−RM17.4M). Growth that pays for itself slowly.

Magnum is almost pure volume: lost ticket sales (−RM12.2M) drove the whole fall; margin and costs barely moved.

MyNews also grew on volume (+RM9.2M), but a thinner margin and the cost of expansion (−RM8.4M) more than erased it.

Two of the three grew revenue; only one kept the profit. The drag is the cost of growth, not demand.
