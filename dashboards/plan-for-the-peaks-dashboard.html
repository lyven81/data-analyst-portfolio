Peak: protect the money

Jun, Jul, Dec

Hold or raise rates, full team, no discounting. The rooms are nearly full, so price is the only way to earn more.

Mid: run as normal

Mar, Apr, May, Aug, Nov

Normal rates and team. Watch bookings and tilt toward peak or quiet actions as the month firms up.

Quiet: improve the empties

Jan, Feb, Sep, Oct

Run promotions and packages, smaller team (about 55%), and do repairs. These are the only months with rooms to fill.
