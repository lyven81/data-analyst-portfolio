Festive peak: win it early

Nov, Dec

Secure stock and book advertising before the peak lands. December is the biggest month of the year, so being stocked and visible early is what wins it.

Steady: plan bigger each month

Mar to Oct

Budget every month for a business about 56% larger than last year. Keep cash ahead of the stock you must buy before the sales arrive.

Post-festive: clear and reset

Jan, Feb

Spending dips after the festive peak. Trim buying, run clearance, and reset stock and plans for the year ahead.
