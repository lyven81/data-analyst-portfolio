Strategic Customer Targeting Recommendations

Target Professional Commuters
Focus marketing efforts on the 34% professional demographic through strategic placement near business districts and commuter routes. Develop loyalty programs specifically designed for busy professionals.
Optimize Morning Operations
Capitalize on the 67% morning sales concentration by ensuring optimal staffing, product availability, and service speed during peak breakfast hours. Consider express lanes for commuters.
Maintain Premium Positioning
Leverage the successful €3.20 price point to reinforce premium quality positioning. Invest in superior ingredients and presentation to justify premium pricing and attract quality-conscious customers.
Expand Customer Base
While maintaining professional focus, develop targeted campaigns for other demographics during off-peak hours. Consider afternoon variants or weekend promotions to diversify revenue streams.
